# Design Team Levels / Titles

<div class="bg-[#f6f8fa] border border-[#e5e7eb] rounded-lg p-6 my-8">
  <p class="text-[17px] leading-7 text-[#1a1f36]">
    A clear understanding of design roles and levels helps establish career paths and set expectations for growth and impact within the organization.
  </p>
</div>

## Individual Contributors

<div class="grid gap-6">
  <div class="bg-white rounded-lg border border-gray-200 p-6">
    <h3 class="text-[15px] font-semibold text-[#1a1f36] mb-2">Junior Designer (IC1)</h3>
    <ul class="list-disc pl-4 space-y-1 text-[14px] text-[#425466]">
      <li>0-2 years of experience</li>
      <li>Learns and applies fundamental design skills</li>
      <li>Works on well-defined, scoped problems</li>
      <li>Collaborates with more senior designers</li>
      <li>Develops understanding of user-centered design</li>
      <li>Builds knowledge of design tools and processes</li>
    </ul>
  </div>

  <div class="bg-white rounded-lg border border-gray-200 p-6">
    <h3 class="text-[15px] font-semibold text-[#1a1f36] mb-2">Designer (IC2)</h3>
    <ul class="list-disc pl-4 space-y-1 text-[14px] text-[#425466]">
      <li>2-5 years of experience</li>
      <li>Works independently on features</li>
      <li>Contributes to design systems</li>
      <li>Collaborates effectively with engineers</li>
      <li>Participates in user research</li>
      <li>Provides feedback to other designers</li>
    </ul>
  </div>

  <div class="bg-white rounded-lg border border-gray-200 p-6">
    <h3 class="text-[15px] font-semibold text-[#1a1f36] mb-2">Senior Designer (IC3)</h3>
    <ul class="list-disc pl-4 space-y-1 text-[14px] text-[#425466]">
      <li>5-8 years of experience</li>
      <li>Leads feature design end-to-end</li>
      <li>Mentors junior designers</li>
      <li>Influences product strategy</li>
      <li>Drives design system improvements</li>
      <li>Plans and conducts user research</li>
      <li>Collaborates across functions</li>
    </ul>
  </div>

  <div class="bg-white rounded-lg border border-gray-200 p-6">
    <h3 class="text-[15px] font-semibold text-[#1a1f36] mb-2">Staff Designer (IC4)</h3>
    <ul class="list-disc pl-4 space-y-1 text-[14px] text-[#425466]">
      <li>8+ years of experience</li>
      <li>Drives major initiatives</li>
      <li>Shapes product vision</li>
      <li>Establishes design patterns</li>
      <li>Leads design system architecture</li>
      <li>Mentors multiple designers</li>
      <li>Influences company strategy</li>
    </ul>
  </div>

  <div class="bg-white rounded-lg border border-gray-200 p-6">
    <h3 class="text-[15px] font-semibold text-[#1a1f36] mb-2">Principal Designer (IC5)</h3>
    <ul class="list-disc pl-4 space-y-1 text-[14px] text-[#425466]">
      <li>10+ years of experience</li>
      <li>Sets design direction</li>
      <li>Influences company strategy</li>
      <li>Recognized industry expert</li>
      <li>Drives innovation and best practices</li>
      <li>Represents company externally</li>
      <li>Leads multiple major initiatives</li>
    </ul>
  </div>
</div>

## Management Track

<div class="grid gap-6">
  <div class="bg-white rounded-lg border border-gray-200 p-6">
    <h3 class="text-[15px] font-semibold text-[#1a1f36] mb-2">Design Manager (M1)</h3>
    <ul class="list-disc pl-4 space-y-1 text-[14px] text-[#425466]">
      <li>Manages small team (3-5 designers)</li>
      <li>Provides direct mentorship</li>
      <li>Coordinates with product/engineering</li>
      <li>Responsible for team output</li>
      <li>Conducts performance reviews</li>
      <li>Manages team resources</li>
      <li>Facilitates team processes</li>
    </ul>
  </div>

  <div class="bg-white rounded-lg border border-gray-200 p-6">
    <h3 class="text-[15px] font-semibold text-[#1a1f36] mb-2">Senior Design Manager (M2)</h3>
    <ul class="list-disc pl-4 space-y-1 text-[14px] text-[#425466]">
      <li>Manages larger team or multiple teams</li>
      <li>Develops team processes</li>
      <li>Influences product roadmap</li>
      <li>Builds team culture</li>
      <li>Manages design managers</li>
      <li>Sets team strategy</li>
      <li>Drives organizational improvements</li>
    </ul>
  </div>

  <div class="bg-white rounded-lg border border-gray-200 p-6">
    <h3 class="text-[15px] font-semibold text-[#1a1f36] mb-2">Design Director (M3)</h3>
    <ul class="list-disc pl-4 space-y-1 text-[14px] text-[#425466]">
      <li>Oversees design organization</li>
      <li>Sets design strategy</li>
      <li>Builds design systems</li>
      <li>Manages design managers</li>
      <li>Drives organizational change</li>
      <li>Represents design at executive level</li>
      <li>Establishes best practices</li>
    </ul>
  </div>

  <div class="bg-white rounded-lg border border-gray-200 p-6">
    <h3 class="text-[15px] font-semibold text-[#1a1f36] mb-2">VP of Design (M4)</h3>
    <ul class="list-disc pl-4 space-y-1 text-[14px] text-[#425466]">
      <li>Executive leadership</li>
      <li>Sets company design vision</li>
      <li>Builds design culture</li>
      <li>Strategic planning</li>
      <li>Manages organization budget</li>
      <li>Drives company-wide initiatives</li>
      <li>External thought leadership</li>
    </ul>
  </div>
</div>
