## Goal Setting Framework

### Vision Setting
- Define 3-5 year career aspirations
- Identify target roles or positions
- Outline desired impact and influence
- Consider industry trends and opportunities

### Skill Development
- Map required competencies
- Plan learning pathways
- Identify mentorship needs
- Schedule training and education

### Impact Planning
- Define desired organizational impact
- Plan leadership contributions
- Set innovation goals
- Outline industry influence

## Implementation Strategy

### 1. Career Milestones
- Set quarterly checkpoints
- Define annual goals
- Plan major transitions
- Identify key achievements

### 2. Learning Path
- Technical skills development
- Leadership capability building
- Industry knowledge expansion
- Network development

### 3. Experience Building
- Project opportunities
- Leadership experiences
- Industry involvement
- Community contribution

## Success Metrics

Track progress through:
- Role advancement
- Skill acquisition
- Project impact
- Team influence
- Industry recognition
